    What is TelkomCodes?

    TelkomCodes is a dynamic learning platform dedicated to advancing coding skills and fostering innovation in the digital age. As an integral part of Telkom Learn, TelkomCodes focuses on providing specialized coding courses and workshops that cater to various levels, from beginners to advanced developers. Our mission is to equip learners with practical coding skills that are essential for thriving in today's technology-driven world.
